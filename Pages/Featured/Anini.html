Anini Itinerary

Day 1: Guwahati/Tezpur to Dibrugarh ▼

Morning: Depart early from Guwahati or Tezpur by bus or private cars.
For Guwahati, the journey to Dibrugarh takes approximately 7-8 hours, and from Tezpur, it’s about 4-5 hours.
Enjoy the scenic countryside views and take a few breaks to stretch and refresh.

Afternoon: Arrive in Dibrugarh and check into your hotel or guesthouse.
After checking in, you can relax or explore the local area.

Evening: Take a walk along the Brahmaputra River, a great place for photos and unwinding.
Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant or in your hotel.

Day 2: Dibrugarh to Roing ▼

Morning: After an early breakfast, depart for Roing, located in the Lower Dibang Valley.
The drive to Roing takes about 5-6 hours through winding roads and beautiful landscapes.
Stop at a few scenic spots or villages along the way to take photos and stretch your legs.

Afternoon: Check into your hotel in Roing.
Visit Ita Pani Hot Springs or Deopani for some relaxation and exploration.

Evening: Enjoy a relaxing evening in Roing with a walk by the river or a quiet spot to unwind.
Dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 3: Roing to Anini ▼

Morning: After breakfast, set off for Anini, the district headquarter of Dibang Valley (approximately 6-7 hours drive).
The road to Anini is more rugged, and you’ll pass through hilly terrain.
Prepare for a bumpy but scenic journey, with chances for photo stops.

Afternoon: Reach Anini and check into your hotel or guesthouse.
Spend some time settling in, and take in the serene atmosphere of this peaceful town.

Evening: Visit the nearby Dibang River for some breathtaking views.
Interact with the locals to learn about the Memba tribe and the culture of the region.
Enjoy dinner and relax for the evening.

Day 4: Exploring Anini ▼

Morning: After breakfast, embark on a local sightseeing tour.
Visit the nearby Mehao Wildlife Sanctuary, which is rich in flora and fauna.
You can also explore Dibang River or take short treks through the hills surrounding the town.

Afternoon: Visit the nearby villages of the Memba tribe to experience local culture, and interact with the indigenous people.
Explore nature spots around the town, including forest trails or river views.

Evening: Relax in Anini, enjoy a local meal, and unwind in the tranquil setting of the town.

Day 5: Anini to Roing ▼

Morning: After breakfast, begin your journey back to Roing.
It will take around 6-7 hours to return, so prepare for another scenic road trip.
Stop at a few places along the way to stretch, have snacks, and enjoy the views.

Afternoon: Arrive back in Roing, check into your accommodation, and take a break.

Evening: Head out for a visit to the Bhismaknagar Fort, an archaeological site with historical significance.
Enjoy some leisure time in Roing, exploring the local food and culture.

Day 6: Roing to Dibrugarh/Tezpur/Guwahati ▼

Morning: After breakfast, you’ll begin your return journey to Dibrugarh, Tezpur, or Guwahati, depending on where you started.
It will take around 5-6 hours to Dibrugarh or 7-8 hours to Guwahati by road.

Afternoon: En route, you can plan stops at small towns or scenic viewpoints to give your group a chance to relax before reaching the destination.

Evening: Reach your destination by evening, and if time allows, you can visit local attractions or enjoy a farewell dinner before your group heads home.
