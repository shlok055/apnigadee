Kaziranga Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati & Travel to Kaziranga ▼

Morning: Arrive at Guwahati Airport (the nearest airport to Kaziranga).

Afternoon: Transfer to Kaziranga National Park (around 5-6 hours drive).

Evening: Check into your resort or lodge near the park.

Relax & Leisure: You can relax at the resort after your long journey, enjoy a nice dinner, and take in the peaceful surroundings.

Day 2: Eastern Range - Jeep Safari & Elephant Ride ▼

Early Morning:
Elephant Safari: Start with a memorable early morning elephant ride in the Eastern Range (Agoratoli) of Kaziranga, known for great wildlife sightings including rhinos, tigers, and various birds.
Enjoy the peaceful ambiance of the park at dawn as you ride through the grasslands and forests on the elephant's back.

Breakfast: Return to the resort for breakfast.
Late Morning: After breakfast, head for a Jeep Safari in the Eastern Range, where you can spot wildlife like the Indian rhinoceros, wild buffalo, swamp deer, and various species of birds.

Afternoon: Return to the resort, relax, and have lunch.

Evening:
Evening Nature Walk: Take a guided nature walk around the resort, where you might spot some smaller wildlife and enjoy the peaceful environment.

Day 3: Central Range - Jeep Safari ▼

Early Morning: Head out for a Jeep Safari in the Central Range (Kohora).
The area is known for sightings of the one-horned rhinoceros, tigers, elephants, and various bird species.

Late Morning: Return to the resort for breakfast and some rest.

Afternoon: After lunch, enjoy some relaxation time.
You may also visit the Kaziranga National Park Museum or local handicraft markets to learn more about the region's culture and history.

Evening: Take a short walk in the vicinity of the park or relax by the resort's pool, enjoying the tranquility of the area.

Day 4: Western Range - Jeep Safari & Elephant Ride ▼

Early Morning:
Elephant Safari: Start your day with an elephant ride in the Western Range of Kaziranga (Bagori).
This range is famous for sightings of tigers, rhinos, and elephants.
The elephant safari offers a unique opportunity to get up close to wildlife in the park.

Late Morning: After the ride, enjoy breakfast at your resort.

Midday: Head out for a Jeep Safari in the Western Range.
Spot tigers, wild boars, wild buffalo, elephants, and various species of deer.

Afternoon: Lunch at the resort and some relaxation time.

Evening: Visit nearby local villages or enjoy a cultural program or folk dance performance (depending on your resort's offerings).

Day 5: Visit to Kaziranga's Outskirts & Relaxation ▼

Morning: After breakfast, take a short excursion to the Mishing village near Kaziranga for a cultural experience.
Learn about the lifestyle, traditions, and crafts of the local people.

Midday: You can also visit the nearby Karbi Anglong Hills, enjoy some scenic views, or take part in birdwatching at nearby areas.

Afternoon: Relax at the resort, enjoy the surrounding nature, or even take a dip in the pool.

Evening: Enjoy a peaceful dinner at the resort, or indulge in a traditional Assamese meal to experience local flavors.

Day 6: Departure ▼

Morning: Depending on your flight timing, enjoy a relaxed breakfast.

Morning/Afternoon: Transfer back to Guwahati (5-6 hours drive).
If time permits, you can stop for a quick visit to Kamakhya Temple or other scenic spots along the way before heading to the airport for your onward journey.

Essential Travel Tips for Kaziranga:

  • Safari Booking: Book the safaris in advance to avoid last-minute hassles, as spots can fill up quickly.
  • Clothing: Wear comfortable clothes suitable for safaris and outdoor activities. Neutral-colored clothing works best for safaris.
  • Camera & Binoculars: Don't forget to bring a camera and binoculars for better wildlife spotting.
  • Weather: The best time to visit Kaziranga is from November to April, as the weather is pleasant for safaris.
  • Local Cuisine: Don't miss trying local Assamese dishes like fish curry, masor tenga (sour fish curry), and pithas (traditional sweets).
