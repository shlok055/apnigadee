Tawang Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati & Drive to Tezpur ▼

Morning: Arrive in Guwahati, and after freshening up, have breakfast.
Begin your drive towards Tezpur (around 5-6 hours drive, 175 km). Tezpur is the perfect base for starting the Tawang journey.

Afternoon: Stop for lunch en route at a roadside dhaba or a restaurant.
Continue the drive to Tezpur, which offers picturesque landscapes and a peaceful atmosphere.

Evening: Upon arrival in Tezpur, check into your hotel.
Explore Tezpur Town and visit Agni Temple or relax near Nameri National Park if time permits. Overnight: Stay in Tezpur.

Day 2: Tezpur to Dirang ▼

Morning: After breakfast, start the drive towards Dirang (around 6-7 hours, 130 km), a beautiful town nestled in the Eastern Himalayas.
You'll cross scenic mountain roads.

Afternoon: Stop for lunch along the way at a local restaurant.
Arrive in Dirang, a picturesque town famous for its natural beauty and ancient monasteries.

Evening: Check into your hotel in Dirang.
Visit the Dirang Dzong, a historical fort.
Explore the Dirang Monastery and take a stroll in the town to experience its tranquility. Overnight: Stay in Dirang.

Day 3: Dirang to Tawang ▼

Morning: After breakfast, begin the drive from Dirang to Tawang (approximately 5-6 hours, 145 km).
This leg of the journey is truly spectacular, as you ascend through high-altitude roads.
En Route: Stop at Sela Pass (13,700 feet), one of the highest motorable roads in the world, offering stunning views of the snow-capped mountains.
Visit Sela Lake, a beautiful high-altitude lake surrounded by alpine meadows.
Continue your drive, passing through scenic valleys, and get glimpses of the magnificent landscapes.

Afternoon: Arrive in Tawang by evening. Check into your hotel and relax after the long drive.

Evening: If time permits, take a walk around the town or visit the Tawang War Memorial, a tribute to the soldiers who lost their lives in the 1962 Sino-Indian war.
Overnight: Stay in Tawang.

Day 4: Explore Tawang ▼

Morning: Start your day by visiting the majestic Tawang Monastery, one of the largest Buddhist monasteries in India. The monastery offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
Explore the Monk’s quarters, the Buddhist scriptures, and the sacred halls.

Afternoon: After lunch, head to Urgyeling Monastery, which is the birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama.
Visit Nuranang Waterfall, a beautiful, serene waterfall located around 40 km from Tawang.

Evening: Enjoy the evening at leisure, exploring the local market in Tawang.
Overnight: Stay in Tawang.

Day 5: Tawang to Dirang ▼

Morning: After breakfast, start your journey to Dirang (approx. 120 km / 4-5 hours).
Dirang is known for its beautiful valleys, hot springs, and Dzong.
Stop at Madhuri Lake (also known as Shungatser Lake) on the way to Dirang.

Afternoon: Check into your hotel in Dirang.
Visit the Dirang Dzong (a traditional fort), and explore the local Buddhist monasteries.
If time permits, visit the Dirang Hot Springs for a relaxing experience.

Evening: Enjoy a quiet evening by the Dirang River and unwind after the long journey.

Day 6: Dirang to Guwahati ▼

Morning: After breakfast, start your return journey to Guwahati (approx. 320 km / 7-8 hours).
Stop for breaks at scenic spots and have lunch en route.

Afternoon: Continue your drive through Assam’s beautiful landscapes and tea gardens.

Evening: Reach Guwahati and, if time permits, relax at a local cafe or explore more of the city before departure.
