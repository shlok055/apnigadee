Mechuka Itinerary

Day 1: Dibrugarh/Tezpur to Along ▼

Morning: Start early from Dibrugarh or Tezpur.
Journey to Along (Aalo) takes approximately 8-9 hours.
Enjoy scenic views of the Brahmaputra Valley.

Afternoon: Arrive in Along and check into your hotel.
Rest and acclimatize to the altitude.

Evening: Explore the local market in Along.
Try local Galo cuisine for dinner.

Day 2: Along to Mechuka ▼

Morning: Early breakfast and departure for Mechuka.
The journey takes 6-7 hours through stunning mountain landscapes.
Pass through beautiful valleys and traditional villages.

Afternoon: Arrive in Mechuka and check into your accommodation.
Initial exploration of the valley.

Evening: Watch the sunset over the snow-capped mountains.
Dinner with local delicacies.

Day 3: Exploring Mechuka ▼

Morning: Visit the 400-year-old Samten Yongcha Monastery.
Explore the ancient prayer wheels and Buddhist artifacts.

Afternoon: Trek to nearby viewpoints for panoramic valley views.
Visit local Memba tribe villages.

Evening: Bonfire and cultural interaction with locals.
Traditional dinner experience.

Day 4: Mechuka Adventures ▼

Morning: Trek to Dorjeeling Village.
Visit the Indo-China border viewing point.

Afternoon: River-side picnic by the Yargyap Chu.
Photography session in the valley.

Evening: Visit local handicraft centers.
Traditional music and dance experience.

Day 5: Mechuka to Along ▼

Morning: Last views of Mechuka Valley.
Start return journey to Along.

Afternoon: Reach Along and rest.
Visit the Darka Village and rice fields.

Evening: Local market shopping for souvenirs.
Farewell dinner.

Day 6: Along to Dibrugarh/Tezpur ▼

Morning: Early departure for return journey.
Photo stops at scenic points.

Afternoon: Continue journey with comfort breaks.

Evening: Arrive at Dibrugarh/Tezpur.
Tour conclusion.
