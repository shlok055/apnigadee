Anini Itinerary

Day 1: Jorhat to Majuli ▼

Morning: Arrive at Jorhat (nearest major town to Majuli).
Take the morning ferry from Nimati Ghat to Kamalabari Ghat (1-hour journey).
Check-in at your homestay or eco-resort.

Afternoon: Visit Kamalabari Satra, one of the prominent Vaishnavite monasteries.
Experience the unique monastery culture and architecture.

Evening: Watch the sunset by the Brahmaputra River.
Enjoy traditional Assamese dinner at your stay.

Day 2: Exploring Satras ▼

Morning: Visit Uttar Kamalabari Satra.
Watch morning prayer ceremonies and mask-making workshops.
Experience traditional Sattriya dance performances.

Afternoon: Visit Auniati Satra, famous for its museum and artifacts.
Witness cultural performances and religious ceremonies.

Evening: Cultural program at local community center.
Traditional dinner with local delicacies.

Day 3: Local Culture & Crafts ▼

Morning: Visit local Mishing tribal villages.
Learn about traditional weaving and textile making.
Experience local pottery making.

Afternoon: Visit mask-making workshops in Samaguri Satra.
Learn about the ancient craft of mask making.

Evening: Shopping for traditional handicrafts.
Cultural performance and dinner.

Day 4: Nature & Departure ▼

Morning: Birdwatching tour in wetlands.
Visit organic farming areas.
Last-minute shopping for souvenirs.

Afternoon: Check-out and take the ferry back to Nimati Ghat.
Depart for Jorhat.

Evening: Reach Jorhat for onward journey.
