Umrangso Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Umrangso ▼

Morning:
Depart from Guwahati/Tezpur early morning.
Journey through scenic hills of Dima Hasao (approximately 6-7 hours).
Enjoy beautiful landscapes and mountain views en route.

Afternoon:
Arrive in Umrangso and check into your accommodation.
Rest and refresh after the journey.

Evening:
Visit the Umrangso Lake viewpoint for sunset.
Dinner at local restaurant featuring Dimasa cuisine.

Day 2: Exploring Umrangso ▼

Morning:
Visit the NEEPCO Hydro Power Project.
Explore the Umrangso Dam area.
Boat ride in Umrangso Lake (subject to permissions).

Afternoon:
Visit local hot springs known for therapeutic properties.
Explore nearby caves and limestone formations.

Evening:
Cultural interaction with local Dimasa community.
Traditional dinner and relaxation.

Day 3: Departure ▼

Morning:
Early breakfast at hotel.
Last-minute shopping for local handicrafts.
Visit to local market if time permits.

Afternoon:
Begin return journey to Guwahati/Tezpur.
Stop at scenic points for photography.

Evening:
Arrive at destination by evening.
