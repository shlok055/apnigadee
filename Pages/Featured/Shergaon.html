Shergaon Itinerary

Day 1: Tezpur to Shergaon ▼

Morning:
Early departure from Tezpur (around 6 AM)
Drive through scenic Bhalukpong and Bomdila
Stop at Bomdila for breakfast and monastery visit

Afternoon:
Reach Shergaon by afternoon (approximately 8-hour drive)
Check-in to homestay/resort
Local village walk orientation

Evening:
Welcome dinner with traditional Sherdukpen cuisine
Cultural introduction by local community members

Day 2: Exploring Shergaon Village ▼

Morning:
Traditional breakfast with local items
Visit to the ancient Shergaon Monastery
Explore traditional Sherdukpen houses

Afternoon:
Visit the local apple orchards (seasonal)
Traditional weaving demonstration
Photography session at viewpoints

Evening:
Bonfire with local stories and legends
Traditional music and dance performance

Day 3: Nature Trail and Waterfalls ▼

Morning:
Trek to nearby waterfalls
Bird watching session
Picnic lunch at scenic spot

Afternoon:
Visit to kiwi farms
Local wine tasting session
Photography at sunset point

Evening:
Traditional cooking class
Dinner with host family

Day 4: Return to Tezpur ▼

Morning:
Early breakfast
Visit local market for souvenirs
Start return journey

Afternoon:
Lunch stop at Bomdila
Continue journey with scenic stops

Evening:
Arrive in Tezpur
Tour conclusion
