Sikkim Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Gangtok ▼

Morning: Arrive at Bagdogra Airport (nearest airport to Sikkim).

Afternoon: Transfer to Gangtok (around 4-5 hours drive). Enjoy scenic mountain views.

Evening: Check into your hotel in Gangtok.

Night: Explore MG Marg, the vibrant heart of Gangtok.

Day 2: Gangtok Local Sightseeing ▼

Morning: Visit Rumtek Monastery and Do-Drul Chorten.

Afternoon: Explore Namgyal Institute of Tibetology and Flower Exhibition Center.

Evening: Visit Hanuman Tok for panoramic views.

Night: Experience local cuisine at MG Marg.

Day 3: Tsomgo Lake & Nathula Pass ▼

Morning: Drive to Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft).

Midday: Visit Nathula Pass (India-China border, permit required).

Afternoon: Enjoy yak rides and snow activities.

Evening: Return to Gangtok.

Day 4: Pelling & West Sikkim ▼

Morning: Drive to Pelling, visit Pemayangtse Monastery.

Afternoon: Explore Khechopalri Lake and Rabdentse Ruins.

Evening: Watch sunset over Kanchenjunga from Pelling.

Day 5: North Sikkim ▼

Morning: Visit Lachung and Yumthang Valley.

Afternoon: Experience Zero Point (weather permitting).

Evening: Return to Gangtok.

Day 6: Departure ▼

Morning: Last-minute shopping at MG Marg.

Afternoon: Transfer to Bagdogra Airport for departure.

Essential Travel Tips for Sikkim:

  • Weather: Pack warm clothes as temperatures can be very cold. Layering is key!
  • Permits: ILP (Inner Line Permit) required for certain areas. Arrange in advance.
  • Altitude: Acclimatize properly to avoid altitude sickness.
  • Best Time: March to May and October to December are ideal.
  • Local Cuisine: Try momos, thukpa, and traditional Sikkimese dishes.
