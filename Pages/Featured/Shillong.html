Shillong Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Shillong ▼

Morning: Arrive at Guwahati Airport (nearest airport to Shillong).

Afternoon: Transfer to Shillong (around 3-4 hours drive). En route, visit the beautiful Umiam Lake.

Evening: Check into your hotel in Shillong.

Night: Explore Police Bazaar, the main shopping area of Shillong.

Day 2: Shillong Local Sightseeing ▼

Morning: Visit Ward's Lake and Lady Hydari Park.

Afternoon: Explore Don Bosco Museum to learn about Northeast Indian culture.

Evening: Visit Shillong Peak for panoramic views of the city.

Night: Experience local music at Cafe Shillong or other music cafes.

Day 3: Cherrapunji Excursion ▼

Morning: Drive to Cherrapunji (2 hours).

Midday: Visit Seven Sisters Falls and Nohkalikai Falls.

Afternoon: Explore Mawsmai Cave and Living Root Bridges.

Evening: Return to Shillong.

Day 4: Mawlynnong & Dawki ▼

Morning: Visit Mawlynnong, Asia's cleanest village.

Afternoon: Experience the crystal-clear waters of Dawki River.

Evening: Return to Shillong via the Bangladesh border view point.

Day 5: Local Culture & Shopping ▼

Morning: Visit the local markets for traditional Khasi handicrafts.

Afternoon: Visit Elephant Falls and Sweet Falls.

Evening: Shopping at Bara Bazaar and local food exploration.

Day 6: Departure ▼

Morning: Last-minute shopping at Police Bazaar.

Afternoon: Transfer to Guwahati Airport for departure.

Essential Travel Tips for Shillong:

  • Weather: Carry both light and warm clothes as weather can be unpredictable. Don't forget rain gear!
  • Footwear: Comfortable walking shoes are essential for exploring the hilly terrain.
  • Photography: Carry your camera for stunning landscapes and waterfalls.
  • Best Time: October to May is the best time to visit Shillong.
  • Local Cuisine: Try Khasi dishes like Jadoh, Tungrymbai, and local black tea.
